---
title: "Generating a Video"
---

1. Select **Generate**.
2. In **Settings**, click the **Model** dropdown.
3. Select **BC8 Anime-Video**.
4. Customize your settings, such as Aspect Ratio, Video Length, and any Advanced Settings you want.
5. Input your prompt or script.
6. Select **Generate**.
7. Preview and download. Once ready, you can preview, edit, or download the video. BC8 also allows you to share the link directly or publish it to social media.

<Info>
Note: Video generation is currently in beta and may not work as expected. Each video costs 30 tokens, and you're limited to one video every 24 hours.
</Info>
