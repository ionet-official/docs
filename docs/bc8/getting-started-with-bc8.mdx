To use BC8, go to [io.net](https://io.net/) and select **BC8.AI**. Click **Join Now**.

<Frame>
![](/images/docs/d6783548c976d5770521e2a8398b02ba9ab9597ad18fd40251e9cfbb52f23d8e-io_bc8_join_now.png)
</Frame>

Sign up using **Google**, **Apple ID**, **X**, **Worldcoin**, or your **Wallet**. Select your preferred option and click **Sign In**.

<Frame>
![](/images/docs/6bffe4dc8d24011b1dec8f3aeca9b5861ebcf5acf0edbbfe9357852a9e4b6ed3-io_bc8_create_universes_text_to_image.png)
</Frame>

Once logged in, you’ll see the BC8 Dashboard, which includes:

* **Image Creation Tool** – Create and manage AI images.
* **Model Creation** – Create your own model with a Trained/Merged model. (Coming Soon!)
* **Explore Generated Ideas** – Browse and engage with pre-built or user-generated images.
* **Settings & Privacy** – Configure memory retention, notifications, and other preferences.
