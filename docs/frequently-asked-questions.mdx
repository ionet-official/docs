title: "Frequently Asked Questions"
---

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What is BC8.ai?">
 BC8.ai is an AI-powered platform that effortlessly creates high-quality, customizable images using advanced, stable diffusion models.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I start creating images with BC8.ai?">
 Log in at bc8.ai, navigate to the "Generate" section, enter your image description, adjust settings like aspect ratio, model, and inference steps, then click "Generate."
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I customize generated images?">
 Yes, BC8.ai allows extensive customization, including prompt tuning, model merging, and advanced configuration settings to meet diverse creative needs.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What settings affect the image generation quality?">
 Key settings include Guidance Scale (adherence to prompts), Inference Steps (refinement steps for quality), Scheduler (noise reduction method), and Seed (ensures reproducibility).
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I view and reuse past prompts or generated images?">
 Yes, past prompts and images can be viewed, edited, reused, or shared directly from your dashboard for convenience and creative continuity.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
